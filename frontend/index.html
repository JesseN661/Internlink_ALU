About InternLink

InternLink is a web-based platform connecting students, small businesses, and universities. Students can discover verified internships, track applications, and receive recommendations based on skills.

SMEs gain access to a pool of motivated students, simplifying recruitment and improving collaboration with local institutions.

Student Profiles

Create skill-based profiles, showcase your experience, and receive internship recommendations that match your abilities.

SME Listings

Post internship opportunities with clear requirements, track applicants, and manage your hiring process online.

Application Tracking

Students can see their application status, and SMEs can review and manage candidates easily.

Notifications

Receive timely alerts for new matches, application updates, and feedback to stay informed at all times.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your account as a Student or SME and complete your profile.

2. Post / Find Internship

SMEs post opportunities; students search or get matched based on skills.

3. Apply / Review

Students apply and track applications; SMEs review candidates efficiently.

4. Get Matched

Receive recommendations and notifications for internships that best fit your profile.
